# Product Philosophy (NexaLedger)

**Purpose (CRITICAL):** Define the operational UX and engineering mindset that every feature must preserve.

**Who uses it:**
- AI (always)
- Human developers (always)

**Change frequency:** Low (only when product direction truly shifts).

**What to include:**
- Calm/premium operational identity
- Clarity & trust over experimentation
- RTL must remain natural
- “Refinement over replacement” principle
- What counts as breaking user trust/UX stability

**What NOT to include:**
- Implementation details
- UI redesign trends
- New frameworks/patterns

---

## Core identity
- NexaLedger is a **calm operational fintech system**.
- It must feel **trustworthy, refined, breathable, and emotionally comfortable**.

## Engineering identity
- **Refinement over replacement**.
- Preserve stable systems: auth, routing, Supabase schema, Electron bridge, session persistence, shared layout foundations.

## UX identity
- Subtle interactions, restrained motion.
- Strong RTL safety—no accidental LTR assumptions.

## Non-goals
- No dashboard-template feeling.
- No flashy trend chasing.
- No experimental redesign culture.
